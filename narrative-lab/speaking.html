Speaking & Lectures

Speaking

Keynotes, invited talks, and workshops on AI, creativity, storytelling, and the future of education.

What Chris talks about

  • AI and human creativity: what changes, what doesn’t
  • Protecting imagination in the age of industrial creativity
  • Narrative innovation in education and immersive media
  • Technology, identity, and digital tragedy
  • Podcasting as research: reaching hard-to-reach audiences
  • How assessment must evolve with generative AI

Who books Chris

Universities, conferences, cultural institutions, festivals, schools, and corporate innovation teams. Audiences range from students and academics to creative professionals and policy-makers.

Talks are research-informed, culturally engaged, and designed to provoke genuine thought rather than deliver platitudes.

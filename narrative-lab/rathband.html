BBC Podcast of the Year

Rathband: A Digital Tragedy

Podcast Arts Council England iTunes #1 2017

A 3-part iTunes podcast designed for visually impaired listenership, exploring the events surrounding PC David Rathband, who was shot and blinded by Raoul Moat in July 2010, and the role social media played in the tragedy that followed.

Funded by Arts Council England, Rathband reached #1 on iTunes and achieved over 30,000 downloads. The BBC described it as “technically stunning.” The production featured Douglas Rushkoff appearing as himself.

Research Questions

  • How does social media mediate trauma, disability, and masculinity?
  • What happens when public and private identity collapse in the digital space?
  • Can digital forces be “tragic” in the classical dramatic sense?
  • How do new narrative forms emerge from new technologies?

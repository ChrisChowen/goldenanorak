The Work

Creative projects spanning podcast drama, memoir, musical theatre, and opera

Each exploring how technology mediates human experience.

Podcast · 2017

Rathband: A Digital Tragedy

A 3-part podcast designed for visually impaired listenership, exploring how social media mediates trauma, disability, and masculinity. Funded by Arts Council England. Featuring Douglas Rushkoff.

BBC Podcast of the Year · iTunes #1 · 30,000+ downloads

Musical · 2019

Cassie & Corey

A podcast musical about eating disorders, created with the NHS Maudsley Hospital and South London D&B crew GTA. Free PSHE resource in 231 schools.

British Podcast Awards Silver & Bronze · 11,000 listens

Memoir · Ongoing

9 Holidays I Wish I Had Never Been On

A 425-page memoir exploring domestic violence and whether AI can create new forms of engagement with trauma through virtual beings.

Palgrave invitation · 328+ Substack subscribers
More Work
Opera Commission

Il Letto

A play about Elvira Bonturi, Puccini's wife. Commissioned by Copenhagen Opera Festival via Helios Collective (2017). The untold story behind one of opera’s greatest composers, through the eyes of the woman who lived with his genius.

Research AI

AI & Memoir Research

Exploring whether a “virtual being” built from memoir text can enhance understanding of trauma. Can a virtual representation of one’s childhood self, constrained in knowledge to a specific timeframe, offer new therapeutic pathways?

Education 2026–2029

MOOC: Narrative Foundations for Immersive Media

Developing a massive open online course on narrative foundations for immersive media and video games, bringing storytelling expertise to emerging interactive formats. Royal Holloway.

TEDx 2017

TEDxUAL

Talk on the Rathband play and the role of social media in mediating tragedy. Exploring how digital forces can be “tragic” in the classical dramatic sense.

Research Strands

Strand 1

New Writing + Emerging Technologies

Pioneering work combining creative writing with emerging tech. From BBC award-winning podcast drama to smartphone musicals, this strand explores how new technologies create new narrative forms and reach audiences that traditional forms cannot.

Strand 2

AI, Creativity & Education

Exploring how AI is reshaping human creativity, how students perceive their own intelligence relative to AI, and how assessment must evolve. Published in Times Higher Education and Wonkhe.

Strand 3

Trauma, Identity & Digital Mediation

How technology mediates human suffering, identity, and connection. From the Rathband tragedy to memoir about childhood domestic violence. What intelligence does trauma create?

Teaching

Notes from the edge of teaching. How creative practice informs pedagogy, what AI is doing to the classroom, and why storytelling might be the most underrated technology in education.

Teaching Philosophy

Teaching is not a side activity. It is the centre of the practice. Every project, every piece of writing, every public argument begins in or returns to the classroom. The best creative work I have made has come from teaching; the best teaching I have done has been informed by making.

I am interested in what happens when you bring real creative technologies—including AI—into the room and let students discover both the power and the limits. Not as evangelism, not as panic, but as critical, hands-on inquiry.
